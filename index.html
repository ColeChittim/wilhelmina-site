Bio

Wilhelmina Mercy is a solo bedroom pop artist based in Seattle, Washington. Their layers of dark ethereal vocals float over heavy bass and flowing synths and violins. Wilhelmina writes, performs, and produces all of their music.Their music laments lost love, and mourns the past, but also looks forward towards the future with fiery passion. Wilhelmina’s debut album is releasing in late May, with singles releasing every few weeks leading up to the big release! Wilhelmina has performed their music at local open mics, and has lately been performing their music on their Twitch channel, wil_wisp. Later, they would love to fill a room with their sound and vibes, but for now they’re vibing on the internet!
